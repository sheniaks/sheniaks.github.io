Где? — Saying Where Things Are

12 min
Teacher: Mike already says куда (где to?) — иду в парк. Today's partner is где (location). The difference is the ending: motion в парк (no change), but location в парке (adds -е). This is the prepositional case.
↗ Куда? — Motion (where to)
go to the park→в парк
go to the garden→в сад
go to work→на работу
📍 Где? — Location (where at)
in the park→в парке
in the garden→в саду
at work→на работе
The pattern: For "where at," most nouns add -е (парк → в парке, работа → на работе). A few short masculine words take -у instead (сад → в саду, угол → на углу) — Mike already said на углу! Same question word где?, same prepositions в/на, just the location ending.
#EnglishRussianPronunciation
1 In summer I spend a lot of time in the garden. Летом я провожу много времени в саду. LYE-tum ya pra-va-ZHOO MNOH-guh VRYE-mee-nee v sa-DOO.
2 Mabo is sleeping in the shade under a tree. Мабо спит в тени под деревом. MA-bo speet v tee-NEE pad DYE-ree-vum.
3 My books are on the shelf in the living room. Мои книги на полке в гостиной. Ma-EE KNEE-gee na POL-kye v gas-TEE-nuy.
4 We are sitting in the garden and drinking tea. Мы сидим в саду и пьём чай. My see-DEEM v sa-DOO ee p'yom chai.

Встречи и планы — Meeting & Plans

8 min
Teacher: Mike noticed the встречать / встретить / встреча family last week. Let's put it to work — making plans to meet people. This introduces a clean aspect pair (process vs. result) without heavy theory.
Word family: root -встреч- — meeting, encounter
встречатьfstree-CHAT'to meet (imperfective)
встретитьFSTRYE-teet'to meet (perfective)
встречаFSTRYE-chaa meeting (noun)
встретитьсяFSTRYE-tee-tsato meet up (with someone)
Imperfective — встречать
Я часто встречаю друзей.
I often meet friends. (repeated, habitual)
Perfective — встретить
Завтра я встречу друга.
Tomorrow I'll meet a friend. (one specific time)
#EnglishRussianPronunciation
9 Let's meet on Saturday. Давайте встретимся в субботу. Da-VAI-tye FSTRYE-teem-sya v soo-BOH-too.
10 Where shall we meet? Где мы встретимся? Gdye my FSTRYE-teem-sya?
11 Let's meet in the park near my house. Давайте встретимся в парке рядом с моим домом. Da-VAI-tye FSTRYE-teem-sya v PAR-kye RYA-dum s ma-EEM DOH-mum.
12 I'm looking forward to our meeting. Я жду нашу встречу. Ya zhdoo NA-shoo FSTRYE-choo.
Where they meet uses "где": Notice sentences 10–11 use the location ending from Part 2 — в парке, not в парк. They'll meet at a location, so it's где. The grammar threads connect.

📚 Домашнее задание — Homework for Mike

  1. Write 4 "где" sentences about where things are in your home and garden — use в/на + the -е ending (в саду, на полке, в гостиной, на газоне).
  2. Describe your garden in summer — 3 sentences. Use: цветы, помидоры, дерево, солнце, тень.
  3. Make a plan: write one sentence inviting someone to meet, using Давайте встретимся в… (day) в… (place).
  4. Practise the pair: Я часто встречаю… (habit) vs Завтра я встречу… (one time).
  5. Translate without looking: "In summer I sit in the garden and read a book in the shade."
