Approach

The Cadmus method in five practical steps.

No 200-page PDFs. No buzzword bingo. Just a structured method that shows you where you stand today, what to prioritize, and how it pays off.

Step 1
Discover — map your risk landscape
We run focused interviews and workshops to map critical assets, key processes, and threat scenarios. The goal: a shared picture of reality, not a theoretical model.
Step 2
Quantify — put numbers behind impact
Using data you already have (and a few targeted assumptions), we translate risk into measurable financial and operational impact.
Step 3
Prioritize — focus on high-leverage moves
Together we build a ranked list of initiatives by risk reduction vs. effort, so your team knows exactly what to do first.
Step 4
Execute — support your teams in delivery
Cadmus can coach, co-deliver, or simply equip your internal teams with clear playbooks, controls, and metrics.
Step 5
Evolve — measure, learn, and iterate
We build feedback loops and dashboards so risk becomes an ongoing, measurable discipline — not a one-off project.
